Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 10, 2025

We collect nothing. Your code never leaves your device.

This is a fully client-side web application. Every comparison, normalization, and URL encoding happens exclusively in your browser using JavaScript. No server receives, processes, stores, or logs any part of your input — ever.

What We Do Not Collect

We intentionally designed this tool to operate with zero data collection

Why No Data Collection?

The primary function of this tool is sensitive: comparing code. We believe absolute privacy is non-negotiable for developers. By performing all computations locally in your browser, we eliminate any risk of server-side data breaches, logs, or third-party access.

Cookies and Tracking

We use no advertising cookies, analytics cookies, or tracking pixels. The only exception is technical cookies used by the hosting platform (if any) to deliver the site, which are outside of our direct control but do not relate to your code input.

Sharing via URL

When you share a comparison link, the URL contains a highly compressed string of your code and settings. This compression happens locally. While the data is private and only readable by the tool, be aware that anyone with the link can access the code you shared.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy. Since the core functionality is client-side, the policy is unlikely to change drastically. The latest effective date will always be displayed at the top of this page.
